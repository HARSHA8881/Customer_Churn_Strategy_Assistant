---
Category: Price Sensitivity
Title: Personalized Discounting
Description: Offer a 15-20% discount on the next billing cycle for users who have shown price sensitivity or have downgraded from a premium tier recently. Highlight the ROI of the higher tier.
---
Category: Low Engagement
Title: Re-engagement Email Sequence
Description: Send a structured 3-part email campaign focusing on newly released features that the user hasn't tried yet. Offer a free 1-on-1 walkthrough with a Customer Success Manager.
---
Category: Product Complexity
Title: Targeted In-App Tutorials
Description: If the user drops off after using a complex feature, present a guided tooltip tour or a quick video tutorial specifically meant to simplify the confusing module.
---
Category: Competitor Risk
Title: Comparison Marketing
Description: If the user mentioned a competitor or falls into a high-risk demographic that competitors target, proactively send a feature matrix showing why our product retains superior value.
---
Category: Lack of Support
Title: Priority Support Escalation
Description: Route any support tickets from this user directly to L2/L3 agents. Minimize wait times to under 1 hour.
---
Category: High Balance / Enterprise Risk
Title: Executive Sponsorship
Description: Assign an executive sponsor to the account. Schedule a quarterly business review (QBR) to align their strategic goals with our product roadmap.
---
Category: Dormant Account
Title: Value Reminder Report
Description: Send an automated "Month in Review" report showing the value they got from the platform. If 0 activity, frame it as "What you missed this month". 
---
Category: Service Disruption
Title: Proactive Apology and Credit
Description: If the user experienced downtime or frequent bugs recently, send an apology email from the engineering team along with service credits to build goodwill.
